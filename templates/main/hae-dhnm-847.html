About the Course

Course Title:

HAE-DHNM-847 – Advanced Human Neurogenetics and Molecular Diagnostics

Overview:

This intensive, research-driven course delves into the molecular basis of neurological diseases, the role of human genetics in diagnosis and treatment, and advanced techniques in molecular diagnostics. Students will examine real case studies, genetic screening tools, and bioinformatics pipelines used in modern medical research.

What You’ll Learn:
  • Human neurogenetics and brain-related gene functions
  • Molecular diagnostic tools and clinical application
  • Next-Generation Sequencing (NGS) for neurological conditions
  • Ethical considerations in genetic testing
  • Integrating genomics with neurobiology

Curriculum

Module 1
– Introduction to Neurogenetics

Module 2
– Genetic Basis of Neurological Disorders

Module 3
– Diagnostic Tools: NGS, Microarrays, qPCR

Module 4
– Bioinformatics in Clinical Neurogenetics

Module 5
– Personalized Medicine and Genetic Counseling

Module 6
– Capstone Project: Case-Based Neurogenetic Analysis
